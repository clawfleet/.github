# ClawFleet

**Your AI bots collaborate like a team. Self-hosted. Free. Open source.**

Stop paying monthly SaaS fees for single-bot AI services. ClawFleet runs a fleet of AI agents on your own hardware — bots that @ each other in Discord, divide tasks, and report back. Managed from a browser dashboard.

### Why ClawFleet?

- **No SaaS subscription** — Use your own API key. Pay per token, not per month
- **Your data stays local** — Conversations, files, memories — all on your disk
- **Bot collaboration** — Your bots @ each other in Discord, automatically dividing work
- **Browser dashboard** — No CLI, no config files. Point-and-click fleet management
- **Always on** — Your Mac runs 24/7, your AI fleet works 24/7
